OUR PRODUCTS

FLORICULTURE SECTOR

Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) calls Indian floriculture a sunrise industry. Indian floriculture industry has been shifting from traditional flowers to cut flowers for export purposes. India exported 20,703 MT of floriculture products to the world, having the major export destinations as U.K, U.S., U.A.E., Germany and Netherlands. Important floricultural crops in the international cut flower trade are rose, carnation, chrysanthemum, gargera, gladiolus, gypsophila, liastris, nerine, orchids, archilea, anthuriu, tulip, and lilies.

Muladhar is a playing as a source to provide the best services for floriculture to reach its maximum potential and serve more nations and its people to gain higher, out of the useful plants.

OUR FARMERS

HYDROPONIC SECTOR

Hydroponic is a subset of hydroculture and is a method of growing plants without soil, using minerals and water solvent. Issues like depleting water resources, are slowly reducing the fertility of soil and because of this very reason, the need to create new and useful methods of cultivation is required in the current world scenario. Hydroponic method is one of the bigger solution and is now considered as a revolutionary method farming.

Currently Muladhar is building the hydroponics channel in India with the capacity of delivering tones of yield per month and further export globally.

HANDICRAFT SECTOR

India is rich in cultural diversity and its heritage, which provides a unique and huge resource for the development of the crafted products. Handicraft industry is one of the important segment of decentralized sector in India and according to the national census of handicraft, the value of handicraft product is nearly INR 26, 213 Crores. Handicrafts are unmatched in India as they represent culture, tradition and heritage of the country.

In the name of keeping Indian traditional products to a higher priority, Muladhar is finding the best solution for representing the hand crafted products to the world. We are having an incubation process of dealing with the local artisans and give them a platform to showcase their hand works while supporting their live hood.
