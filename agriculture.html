AGRICULTURE

Improving global food protection, especially in the light of climate change, means that the necessary crops have to be produced in more productive areas and food needs to shift from abundant to scarce areas.

The world's growing demand for food can be fulfilled by agriculture. Improving global food protection, especially in the light of climate change, means that the necessary crops have to be produced in more productive areas and food needs to shift from abundant to scarce areas. Farmers will be given the resources to improve production and satisfy potential food demand through expenditure on creativity and training. We recognize that the farmers of the planet are robust enterprises. We enable farmers at all production stages to adjust themselves to changing environments, market preferences, and dietary needs.
